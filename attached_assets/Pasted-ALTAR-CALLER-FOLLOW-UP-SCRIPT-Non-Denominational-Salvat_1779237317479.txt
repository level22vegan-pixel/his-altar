ALTAR CALLER FOLLOW-UP SCRIPT
Non-Denominational · Salvation & First-Time Decisions
==================================================


📞 MAIN CALL SCRIPT
----------------------------------------

Warm Opening:
"Hi, may I speak with [First Name]? … Hi [Name]! My name is [Your Name], and I'm a part of the care team at [Church Name]. How are you doing today?"

[If they ask who's calling:]
"I'm a volunteer from [Church Name] — we just wanted to reach out personally and see how you're doing. Do you have just a couple of minutes?"

Reference the Moment:
"We noticed that you came forward during our service on [Day/Sunday], and we just want you to know — that step you took was a big deal. We're really glad you were there."

"Coming forward takes courage, and it means a lot to our whole church family that you did."

Open-Ended Check-In:
"I'd love to hear — how have you been feeling since Sunday? Has anything stood out to you?"

[If they're quiet:]
"Sometimes after a moment like that, people feel a mix of things — peace, excitement, maybe even some questions. Is any of that resonating with you?"

Affirm Their Decision:
"What you did on Sunday — giving your life to Jesus — that is the most important decision anyone can ever make. Heaven is celebrating you right now. Literally."

"This is just the beginning of an incredible journey, and you don't have to figure it out alone. That's exactly why we wanted to call."

Offer Next Steps:
[New Believers Class:]
"We have a free class called [Class Name] that's just for people in your exact shoes. It's super casual, no pressure — just a great way to get some foundations. Would that be something you'd be open to?"

[Connect to Community:]
"We'd also love to connect you with someone who can just be a friend and answer any questions you have along the way. Is that something you'd want?"

[Invite Back:]
"And of course, we'd love to see you back this Sunday. Is there anything that would make it easier for you to come?"

Offer to Pray:
"Before I let you go — would it be alright if I said a quick prayer for you right now over the phone? It'll only take a minute."

Warm Close:
"[Name], it was so good talking to you today. You matter to us, and you matter to God. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you ever need anything — we're here for you."

"I'll [send you a text / follow up next week] with info on [next step mentioned]. We're rooting for you!"


🔀 BRANCH SCENARIOS
----------------------------------------

If They Don't Remember Coming Forward:
"No worries at all! We just like to reach out to anyone who visited or connected with us. We're glad you came. Is there anything we can do for you or pray about with you today?"

If They Seem Hesitant or Guarded:
"I totally understand — I just want you to know there's zero obligation here. We're not calling to add you to a list or ask anything of you. We genuinely just wanted to check on you as a person. That's it."

[If still resistant:]
"That's completely okay. I'll let you go — but I do want you to know our doors are always open, and you're always welcome."

If They're Going Through a Crisis:
"I'm so glad you shared that with me. That sounds really hard, and I don't want to rush past it. Can you tell me a little more about what's been going on?"

[To escalate:]
"I want to make sure you get the right support. Would it be okay if I connected you with one of our pastors? They're really great and would love to talk with you personally."

If They're Excited & Engaged:
"I love that! That joy you're feeling — that's real. That's the Holy Spirit. Let's make sure we keep that momentum going. Can I get you connected with some things that will really help you grow?"

Voicemail Script:
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from [Church Name]. I just wanted to reach out personally and say how glad we are that you were with us on Sunday. You matter to us and we're thinking of you. Feel free to call or text me back at [number] anytime — no pressure at all. Hope to connect with you soon. God bless!"


🙏 CLOSING PRAYERS
----------------------------------------

Prayer for a New Believer:
"Father, we come to You right now just so grateful for [Name]. Lord, You knew them before they were born, and You drew them to Yourself on Sunday — and we thank You for that. We pray that the decision they made becomes a foundation that nothing can shake. Fill them with Your peace, Your joy, and Your presence. When questions come, let them feel You close. Surround them with people who will love them well and walk alongside them. And Lord, let this be the beginning of the most incredible story — one where they look back and say 'that was the day everything changed.' In Jesus' name, amen."

Prayer for Someone in Pain:
"Lord, right now I lift [Name] to You. You see every single thing they're carrying, and You care about every piece of it. I pray that right now, in this moment, they feel Your hand on them — that they know they are not alone, not forgotten, and not without hope. Give them supernatural strength for what's ahead. Send them the right people, the right help, and the right words at exactly the right time. You are a good Father, and I trust You with [Name]. In Jesus' name, amen."

Short General Prayer:
"Lord, thank You for [Name]. I pray right now that wherever they are, whatever they're facing, they feel Your love surrounding them. Keep them close to You. Guide their steps. And let what happened on Sunday be something they carry with them for the rest of their life. In Jesus' name, amen."
